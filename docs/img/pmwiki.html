Directory of Research Projects:

The following is a list of research projects that are housed in this site.

Current research activities

  • ThaiBehaviorDrivenDev - Use of Thai version of Cucumber to include end-user in behavior driven design and software testing
  • GraphicProgDesign - Use of yEd as a Ruby CASE tool
  • ThaiLectureSlides - Development of Thai lecture slides and handouts in Thai using the Beamer compiled under XeLaTeX
  • ThaiMetafont - Redevelopment of a Thai Metafont lost 20 years ago
  • CSRetainology - Implementation and calibration of a Thai student retention system within the Faculty of Science
  • FreshmenCompSci - Improving interest, grades and retention of freshmen computer science students using Scratch, Processing and various active learning techniques
  • PhotoFeatureMorph - preliminary mathematical studies to identify a feature in a photograph and replace it with another.
  • UndergradCSEd - An attempt to describe and align the philosophical goals of students with the expectations of the professional community
  • InterCollegeDevel - A project to develop an international Research and Development Program at Payap University International College
  • InterCollegeWeb - A project workspace for developing ideas for the International College Website
